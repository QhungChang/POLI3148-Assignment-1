# Note on AI Use

This project made use of AI tools, primarily ChatGPT and Google Gemini, to support different stages of the analysis and report writing process.

Google Gemini (2.5 Flash), integrated within Google Colab, was mainly used for coding assistance. It was helpful in suggesting, debugging, and refining Python code, particularly for data processing and generating visualisations used in the analysis.

ChatGPT was used in several ways. First, it assisted in suggesting potential directions for analysis related to demonstration events. Second, it helped interpret results from the data. For example, when an apparent discrepancy was observed in the timing of protest spikes, ChatGPT suggested that this could be due to ACLED’s weekly data aggregation, which limits temporal precision. Finally, ChatGPT was used to improve the clarity of writing by correcting grammatical errors and refining sentence structure in the report.

All analysis, interpretation, and conclusions were reviewed and verified by the author.
